About

SUPRA SAEINDIA

SUPRA SAEINDIA is an event for the engineering students all over India. As an event that challenges young minds to compete as teams in designing a Formula category vehicle, there is no other event like this in the country. Developing a vehicle consisting of various subsystems, meeting engineering specifications, timelines and above all meeting safety standards is a challenge not for the faint hearted. A one year long challenge across multiple stages comprising of design defence, it has multiple rounds of technical inspections and actual construction of the vehicle. The event encompasses all elements of vehicle building from aesthetics in design, to the choice of parts, through structure design and modelling, to fabrication and testing. It sets the stage to assess the capability for teamwork, entrepreneurship, leadership and management skills of the students. Teams constituting the crème de la crème, selected on criteria such as Engineering Design, Design Validation, Project plan, from various Engineering Colleges across the country, strive for a place on the podium.

Formula Student

Formula Student or FS, is an international design based competition for engineering students. Teams from several parts of the world compete in this event. The event is won by the team which not just makes the fastest car, but the one with the best overall package of performance, construction and sales planning. Team members are urged to go the extra step by incorporating experience in building and manufacturing along with the economic aspects. The car should have great driving characteristics, like braking, acceleration and handling. It should also be reliable. The business viability of the project is also of utmost importance. The jury judges teams based on the car and sales plan, ie, construction, cost planning and sales presentation apart from the performance on track.
